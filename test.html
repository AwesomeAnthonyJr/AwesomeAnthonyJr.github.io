Introduction

WAKE UP.YOU'VE BEEN HERE BEFORE.YOUR MEMORY HAS BEEN STOLEN.YOU LEFT THIS MESSAGE.

Waking up in a steel cage, you find a message you left yourself. You are a magician and you must reclaim your memories. Welcome to Mage Hand. As a magician, knowledge is power. Throughout over the course of your memory-seeking journey, you will learn how to cast up to 40 different spells, and though you may be defeated, if you can remember them, the spells will work for you on another attempt. So go forth through the manor, with just one goal in mind:

RECLAIM YOUR MEMORY.

Controls

Use the number keys to move the corresponding fingers up or down.

PAUSE

The "!" symbol signifies that you can interact by pressing the Q key. By interacting, keys can be picked up, buttons can be pressed, doors can be entered, and scrolls can be read.

By pressing the Spacebar, the current hand position is stored in the sequence and the hand is closed. The current sequence of positions is displayed above the hand. By putting the right hand-position sequences together, you can cast any spell in the game.

*Note that attempting to store with a fist will clear the sequence.

With a sequence ready, press the E key to cast it as a spell. If the sequence does not make a legitimate spell, then nothing will happen.

Use the arrow keys to move.
