LeafLight Studios

About

At LeafLight Studios we are dedicated to creating the coolest games we can, and to do that, it takes people. Meet our team, the rotating cast of artists, programmers, musicians, and all-around creative people who make LeafLight Studios what it is.

Anthony D. Salsbury

Founder & Creative Director

LeafLight Studios has been my dream to bring to light for years, and I'm proud to say it's finally real. It began with wanting to create games I wanted to play, but has since grown into a studio focused on pushing the boundaries in game design and making new creative things. I love what I do here and want the studio to become the best it can be.

- Anthony D. Salsbury
